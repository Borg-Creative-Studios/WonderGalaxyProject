About the Wonder Galaxy Project

Our mission is to educate and inspire the next generation of astronauts, engineers, astronomers, and barrier breakers through interactive experiences.

The Wonder Galaxy Project is in its early stages of development, but we are shooting for the stars. 

The experience

The experience we will deliver will utilize some of today's latest technology as well as incorporating retro presentation techniques in a newly reimagined way to present the aerospace industry's WONDER in a manner that will be enjoyed by all ages.
